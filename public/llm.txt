# Amana Compliance
> AML compliance consultancy for HMRC-supervised UK businesses

## Overview

Amana Compliance builds bespoke anti-money laundering (AML) compliance frameworks for small and medium UK businesses supervised by HMRC. The founder has 10+ years experience in tier-1 banking compliance (Deutsche Bank, NatWest, SMBC Nikko, Ebury).

## Services

Primary service: The Inspection-Ready System

- Bespoke risk assessment (specific to firm, area, client mix, transaction types)
- AML policy and procedures manual
- Customer due diligence (CDD) checklists
- Sanctions and PEP screening setup
- Nominated officer framework
- Staff AML training with certificates

Included at no extra cost:

- HMRC inspection file (tabbed and indexed)
- Inspection day playbook
- New joiner induction pack
- Suspicious activity toolkit
- 90-day support line
- Quarterly regulatory alerts

Guarantee: 12-month workmanship guarantee. If HMRC identifies any deficiency in the delivered framework within 12 months, Amana rebuilds it at no cost and provides a full day of remediation time.

## Target Clients

HMRC-supervised businesses required to register for AML supervision:

- Estate agents
- Letting agents (since May 2025, must screen against UK sanctions list)
- Accountants (ASP registration required)
- High-value dealers (accepting €10K+ cash payments: jewellers, car dealers, antique dealers, auction houses)
